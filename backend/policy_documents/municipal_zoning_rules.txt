[Municipal Zoning Ordinance Section 204-IZ - Industrial Zoning Limits]
Establishes the municipal boundaries and air quality requirements for industrial facilities.
Under Section 204-IZ, the maximum allowable concentrations at the property line of any industrial complex are:
- Sulfur Dioxide (SO2): 75.0 ppb (parts per billion) averaged over 1 hour.
- Fine Particulate Matter (PM2.5): 35.0 ug/m3 (micrograms per cubic meter) over 24 hours.
Any exceedance recorded by municipal sensor nodes surrounding a facility plot constitutes a zoning violation.
Upon detection of critical exceedances:
- The city must issue an immediate enforcement warning.
- The facility is legally required to implement a data-hold order on all emissions data, process logs, and maintenance logs.
- Field verification teams must be dispatched to coordinates for site audits.
- Corporate owners must submit a corrective action plan within 10 business days of notification.
